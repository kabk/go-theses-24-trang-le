However, to the strangers on the street, balcony is a fascinating pop-up stage that they habitually gaze up to to satisfy their curiosity. Whatever the owner do: sitting, reading, smoking, or simply standing, their activities represent a glimpse of the life the stranger will never live in. This archive of balconies everywhere across the continents presents the tension between privacy of balcony’s owner and the gazes of the strangers passing by the balcony.
